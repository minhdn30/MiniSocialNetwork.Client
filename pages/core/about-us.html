Why CloudM exists

Designed for the way people actually keep in touch

CloudM brings posts, stories, highlights, and messaging into one place so the social experience feels connected instead of fragmented.

The goal is simple: give people a space that feels expressive and modern while still staying comfortable to use every day.

What you can do on CloudM right now
  • Browse your feed to catch posts and fresh updates from people you care about
  • Chat with your friends in a way that feels familiar and comfortable
  • Share your moments through stories and posts
  • Check notifications and follow suggestions so you do not miss the connections worth noticing
